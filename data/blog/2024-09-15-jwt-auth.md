This post covers implementing JWT with refresh tokens in a MERN stack app, secure storage strategies, rotation, and revocation.

Highlights:

- Keep access tokens short-lived and store refresh tokens in HttpOnly cookies.
- Implement token rotation to reduce the risk of replay attacks.
